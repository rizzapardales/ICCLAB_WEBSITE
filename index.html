PHUM VIPHURIT


Phum, is a Thai singer-songwriter. His music demonstrates influences from various genres. My favorite music of his is Lover boy, Long gone, Strangers in a dream, run, and sweet hurricance.

Munimuni are a Filipino indie folk band from Quezon City, Philippines. The band consists of Adj, John Owen, Jolo, and Josh. My favorite music of theirs are Sayo, Solomon, and Banaag.
